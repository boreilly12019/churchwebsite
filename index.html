Our Mission

To connect people with Jesus, strengthen families, and make disciples.

We affirm that West Boca Baptist Church holds to the historic Baptist faith and doctrine, grounded in the Holy Scriptures, and aligned with orthodox evangelical and Baptist beliefs.

"If you confess with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved."

Romans 10:9 (CSB)

Connecting people with Jesus through authentic worship and community.

Strengthen

Strengthening families by providing biblical guidance and support for all ages.

Disciple

Making disciples who are grounded in the Holy Scriptures and Baptist faith.
